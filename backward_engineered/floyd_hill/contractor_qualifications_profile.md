## Relevant CDOT Experience

Kraemer has delivered multiple large CDOT projects in the I-70 Mountain Corridor and along the Front Range:

### I-70 Veterans Memorial Tunnels Expansion
- **Scope:** Widened the Veterans Memorial Tunnels (formerly Twin Tunnels) on I-70 near Idaho Springs to add a third eastbound lane
- **Value:** ~$70M
- **Relevance:** Same I-70 corridor, tunneling and bridge work in constrained mountain environment, traffic management on active I-70

### I-25 North Express Lanes
- **Scope:** Added express toll lanes to I-25 between US 36 and SH 7 in the northern Denver metro area
- **Value:** ~$700M+
- **Relevance:** Large-scale CM/GC delivery, complex phasing on active interstate, toll infrastructure

### I-25 South Gap
- **Scope:** Widened I-25 between Castle Rock and Monument, adding express lanes through a mountainous segment
- **Value:** ~$350M
- **Relevance:** Mountain corridor construction, rock excavation, wildlife crossings, environmental sensitivity
