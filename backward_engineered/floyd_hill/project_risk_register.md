# I-70 Floyd Hill — Project Risk Register

*Backward-engineered from project scope, environmental documents, and construction challenges identified during research. This represents the type of risk register CDOT would develop during project planning and share with CM/GC proposers.*

---

## Risk Rating Scale

**Likelihood:** Low (1) | Medium (2) | High (3)
**Impact:** Low (1) | Medium (2) | High (3) | Very High (4)
**Risk Score:** Likelihood × Impact

---

## Geotechnical Risks

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Score | Mitigation |
|----|------|-----------|--------|-------|------------|
| G-1 | Unforeseen rock conditions differ from boring data, requiring modified blast designs | 2 | 3 | 6 | Additional geotechnical investigation during preconstruction; adaptive blast design protocols |
| G-2 | Rock slope instability after excavation | 2 | 4 | 8 | Real-time slope monitoring; shotcrete stabilization; rock bolt installation as preventive measure |
| G-3 | Excessive over-break during blasting increases material quantities | 3 | 2 | 6 | Pre-split blasting techniques; electronic detonators for precision timing; contingency quantities |
| G-4 | Foundation conditions for new bridges differ from design assumptions | 1 | 4 | 4 | Foundation verification borings during preconstruction; design flexibility for foundation types |
| G-5 | Rockfall hazard to traffic and workers during and after excavation | 2 | 4 | 8 | Scaling operations, catch fences, rockfall barriers; post-blast inspections before reopening lanes |
